The problem

People connect to your building, your programs, your personalities. All of that can change overnight.

There's nothing wrong with a great facility, a beloved preacher, or a killer student ministry. But when those are the primary reasons people attend, connection stays consumeristic — and fragile. The deeper invitation is to connect people to the purpose your church exists for: a unique, disciple-making mission and vision.

Most leadership teams have never been given the tools — or the guided time together — to do that work. That's exactly what the co::Lab is for.
